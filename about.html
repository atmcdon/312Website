About Next Week Movies

Next Week Movies is a unique online platform designed for movie enthusiasts who are eager to discover and discuss their favorite films. Our mission is to foster a diverse and inclusive community where people can suggest, explore, and engage in meaningful conversations about the movies they love.

Every Friday at 12AM PST, we generate movie suggestions based on the weekly movie suggestion forms submitted by our userbase. These forms allow users to share their opinions on what movies should be highlighted for the following week, giving them an active role in shaping the recommendations.

In our Discussions page, users can express their opinions by leaving comments and providing personal ratings for the highlighted movie/s of the week. To maintain a respectful and inclusive environment, users are required to create an account using their email or phone number before participating in comments, ratings, or submitting suggestion forms.

We believe that movies have the power to ignite discussions and create connections. Andrew and Thomas, the owners of Next Week Movies, are passionate about films and the conversations they can inspire. Their appreciation for movies motivated them to create this website, aiming to establish a friendly online environment where individuals are free to share their opinions and discuss various aspects of film.

We would like to express our gratitude to our userbase for their continued support and active participation in the weekly discussions. If you haven't joined yet, we encourage you to get started and share your thoughts. We hope you have a fantastic time exploring movies and engaging in discussions on Next Week Movies!

About the Owners

Next Week Movies is owned and operated by Andrew and Thomas, who have a deep appreciation for movies. They understand the power of cinematic experiences and how they can spark meaningful discussions.
